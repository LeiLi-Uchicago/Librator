NOTE

We identify residue numbering by aligning query sequence against a few templates from a previous study (https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25391151/)

Our templates are as same as those used in a FLU DB implementation (https://www.fludb.org/brc/haNumbering.spg?method=ShowCleanInputPage&decorator=influenza)

However, due to the different aligners used in our software (muscle) and FLU DB (not clear), we do observe inconsistent results between Librator or FLU DB when aligning a query sequence against a template (or multiple templates) of different subtypes. The reason of this inconsistency is that different aligners usually use different algorithms, more specifically, determining where to open gaps in the alignment. So please double check your result when aligning your sequence to a template of a different subtype.
